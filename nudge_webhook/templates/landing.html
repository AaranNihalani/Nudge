Key Findings

Who gets left out of formal credit

Each figure below is an average marginal effect from logit regression with district fixed effects — the change in probability of accessing formal credit for that group, holding all else equal. Each estimate is independent; they are not additive.

Effect Group What it means
−10.7 pp Muslim vs Hindu The largest religion-linked gap in the data. Holds after controlling for income, location, and household size.
−6.2 pp SC vs OBC Scheduled Caste households face a significant gap even after income and geography are controlled for.
+6.2 pp Upper caste vs OBC The gap runs in both directions — caste predicts credit access independently of income.
−3.7 pp ST vs OBC Scheduled Tribe households, where geographic remoteness and social exclusion compound each other.
−3.2 pp Christian vs Hindu A smaller but statistically significant gap, likely reflecting regional concentration patterns.
+2.5 pp Per acre of land owned Land ownership functions as implicit collateral, which regulated lenders weigh more heavily than informal ones.
+2.3 pp Urban vs rural Proximity to bank branches and MFI offices still matters, even after district-level controls.
+1.1 pp Per ₹1,000 monthly income Income is the strongest individual-level predictor — each extra ₹1,000 per month per person raises formal credit likelihood.
−0.6 pp Per additional household member Larger households are marginally less likely to access formal credit, possibly reflecting income instability or lower per-capita collateral.
The Research

Built on peer-reviewed findings

Nudge is the applied counterpart to published research on India's credit market. When you share your household profile, Nudge uses the paper's estimates to surface alternatives that match your situation — not generic advice.

How it works

Three steps

01
Tell Nudge about your loan
Set your district, then describe the loan in plain English — amount, time, and lender type. Nudge also asks six quick questions about your household to personalise the guidance. You can skip any of them.
02
See what it actually costs
Nudge computes the total interest, total repayment, and monthly payment in rupees for your loan — and compares it against regulated MFI rates available in your district, ranked by APR.
03
Make an informed decision
Nudge shows you regulated alternatives with contact details. Your profile is remembered — next time you open the chat, you pick up where you left off. No sign-up required.
